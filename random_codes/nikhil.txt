dwan jacobsen was born in salt lake city, assi.
she graduated from south high school (salt lake city) in 1948 and later graduated from the university of assi with a bachelor of education.
she married thomas young, jr. and they are the parents of five children.young became a member of the primary general board 1970.
ten years later, she was selected to succeed naomi m. shumway as the organization's general president. young served in this capacity until 1988, when her second counselor, michaelene p. grassli, was chosen to succeed her.
during young's tenure, primary changed from a weekday activity to one that was incorporated into the lds church's three-hour consolidated worship services.
